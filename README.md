&nbsp;Staffing Optimization

Finding: 1 AM - 5 AM accounts for only 2% of daily orders (92% below average)



Recommendation: Reduce overnight staffing to skeleton crew



Annual savings: $27,375



Risk: May impact 24/7 delivery promise



Next step: 3-month pilot with delivery time tracking



Customer Profitability

Finding: Not all segments are equal



Segment	Profit/Order	% of Total Profit

Gold	$16.50	42%

Silver	$6.80	35%

Bronze	$2.10	23%

Insight: Gold customers (15% of base) drive 42% of profit. Bargain hunters lose money in Q1.



Action: Shift marketing budget toward Gold-acquisition campaigns



&nbsp;Referral Program ROI

Finding: Program pays for itself in 3.4 months



Metric	Value

Discounts given (2024)	$287,450

Referred customer value	2.3x higher

Payback period	3.4 months

5-year projected value	+$1.2M

Decision: Keep the program. Test tiered rewards (10% first, 5% second referral).



&nbsp;Black Friday Impact

Finding: 8.5x revenue lift, but varies by segment



Segment	Lift vs Normal Day

Fashion Shoppers	12x

Electronics	9x

Bargain Hunters	3x

Action: Segment Black Friday emails — send fashion-specific offers to Fashion Shoppers 24 hours early.



&nbsp;Geographic Focus

&nbsp;78% of profit comes from 3 regions



Region	Profit Share	Avg Order

West	32%	$98

Northeast	28%	$87

Central	18%	$76

Insight: Southeast has customers but 35% lower order value — investigate before investing.



2026 Priority: Increase West marketing by 20%, diagnose Southeast underperformance.
